# Project Roadmap

This document outlines the planned features and improvements for the D&D Character Sheet Manager. The roadmap is divided into several key areas, focusing on enhancing gameplay, leveraging AI, fostering collaboration, and allowing for greater customization.

---

## Phase 1: Core Gameplay and Character Management

This phase focuses on features that directly improve the experience of using the character sheet during a game session.

* **[ ] Interactive Dice Roller**
    * **Concept:** Integrate a dice roller directly into the character sheet. Users can click on an ability score, skill, or attack to automatically roll the appropriate dice.
    * **Benefit:** Streamlines gameplay by eliminating the need for physical dice or a separate dice-rolling app.

* **[ ] Automated Leveling Up**
    * **Concept:** Create a "Level Up" wizard that guides users through advancing their character. It will automatically update hit points, proficiency bonuses, and prompt users to select new spells and features based on their class.
    * **Benefit:** Simplifies a complex process, especially for new players, and reduces the chance of errors.

* **[ ] Active Effects and Condition Tracking**
    * **Concept:** Implement a system to track conditions (e.g., "Blinded," "Poisoned") and other temporary effects. Applying a condition will automatically update the character's stats and rolls.
    * **Benefit:** Makes it easier to manage combat as the sheet dynamically reflects the character's current state.

---

## Phase 2: AI-Powered Creative Tools

This phase aims to integrate generative AI to assist both players and Dungeon Masters with creative aspects of the game.

* **[ ] Adventure Log with AI Scribe**
    * **Concept:** Add an "Adventure Log" for session notes. An integrated "AI Scribe" (using the Gemini API) can expand on notes, create narrative summaries, or generate plot hooks based on the log's content.
    * **Benefit:** Creates a living document of the campaign and provides a fun, interactive way to chronicle adventures.

* **[ ] NPC and Quest Generators**
    * **Concept:** Leverage the Gemini API to create on-the-fly generators for Non-Player Characters (NPCs) and quests.
    * **Benefit:** An invaluable tool for DMs, helping them to improvise and enrich their campaigns.

---

## Phase 3: Social and Collaborative Features

This phase will transform the application from a solo tool into a collaborative platform for entire gaming groups.

* **[ ] Campaign Management**
    * **Concept:** Allow users to create "Campaigns" and invite other users to join. Within a campaign, players can view each other's characters, and the DM will have a special dashboard.
    * **Benefit:** Turns the app into a central hub for D&D groups.

* **[ ] Character Sharing and Privacy Controls**
    * **Concept:** Enable users to share their character sheets via a public or private link with different permission levels (e.g., "view only," "full edit access").
    * **Benefit:** Makes it easy for players to share characters with their DM or friends.

* **[ ] DM-Specific Tools**
    * **Concept:** Create a suite of tools for Dungeon Masters within the campaign view, such as a campaign calendar, a shared document for notes, and private messaging.
    * **Benefit:** Makes the application an all-in-one solution for running a D&D game.

---

## Phase 4: Customization and Expansion

This phase focuses on giving users the tools to add their own content to the application.

* **[ ] Homebrew Content Creator**
    * **Concept:** Allow users to create their own custom content, such as homebrew races, classes, spells, and items, and use it in their character sheets.
    * **Benefit:** Provides a high degree of flexibility and allows users to tailor the application to their specific needs and playstyles.
